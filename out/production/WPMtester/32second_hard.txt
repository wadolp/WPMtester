The night was thick with fog; the air felt heavy. Jason stared out of the window, wondering, “How much longer can this storm last?” The wind howled, rattling the glass—was it a sign of something? Suddenly, lightning flashed, followed by thunder. “Why does it always feel like there’s more to these storms?” he muttered, as if expecting an answer. The phone rang, breaking the silence. He jumped, grabbed the receiver, and answered, “Hello?”
